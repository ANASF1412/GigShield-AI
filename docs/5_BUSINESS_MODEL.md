# Business Model & Economics

## 💰 The Challenge with B2C Parametrics
Gig workers in SEA operate on extremely tight margins. A flat ₹45/week standalone insurance subscription presents astronomical customer acquisition cost (CAC) and immense churn vectors.

## 🤝 The B2B2C Pivot (Embedded API)
JARVIS EnviroSense is not meant to be a standalone app downloaded by a driver. It is an **Embedded Insurance API**. 
*   **Target Market:** Delivery Platforms (Zomato, Swiggy, Zepto, Uber).
*   **The Swap:** Aggregators integrate the JARVIS API. The ₹45/week premium translates to a ₹6/day invisible surcharge embedded inside surge-delivery fees during rain.
*   **ROI for Platforms:** Resolves driver strike rates, minimizes churn, and boosts active fulfillment volume during volatile weather when competitors' drivers go offline.

## 📊 Solvency Protection
*   **Pre-Loop Exposure Calculation:** A math gate prevents payouts if total liabilities breach 40% of the aggregate pool.
*   **NCB Structure:** Good behavior systematically reduces liability footprint.
