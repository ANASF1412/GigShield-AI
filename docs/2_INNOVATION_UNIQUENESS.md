# Why We Are Unique

## ⭐️ Category Defining Architecture
Instead of building a streamlined app to "make filing claims easier," we fundamentally eliminated the concept of filing a claim.

### Pre-Loop Fairness Gate
Traditional algorithmic insurers loop sequentially, meaning if disaster breaks the pool, users 1-50 get paid and 51-100 get nothing. JARVIS mathematically anticipates total disaster exposure BEFORE execution, routing everyone to manual review equitably if the payload breaches 40% liability.

### Dual-Layered AI
We do not trust AI to issue money. We use Machine Learning (Random Forests / Isolation Forests) securely to gauge risk factors and anomalies. We then use LLMs (Gemini) strictly as an *Explainable Translator* restricted precisely to outputting JSON logs.

### Proactive Warnings
Insurance is meant to recover loss. We actively prevent it. By utilizing predictive LLM forecasts on OpenWeather payloads, the app advises workers to take shelter actively saving capital.
